💻 How to Use

Once you install the program, open it from your desktop shortcut.

Authentication

The program will ask you to log in to your Telegram account. Enter your phone number and confirm the login code sent to your Telegram app. This creates a secure connection so the software can perform its tasks.

Keyword Searches

Enter your search terms into the input field. The software scans chats you belong to and finds matching results. It saves these results to a database on your computer.

Data Export

You can export member lists from groups. Select the group you intend to analyze and click the export button. The tool creates a file you can open in spreadsheet software.

Privacy Features

You can route your traffic through a proxy or Tor if you prioritize anonymity. Go to the settings menu and update your network configuration to match your proxy settings.

🛡️ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this software safe? Yes, this tool runs locally on your machine. We do not transmit your login tokens to external servers. Your data stays on your computer.

What if the search returns no results? Ensure your Telegram account has access to the chats you want to search. The tool only sees messages you have permission to view.

Can I use this commercially? The software is intended for research and intelligence work. Always follow local laws and the Telegram terms of service when you gather data.
